About Us

HNS Universal Enterprise is an ambitious and motivated startup company. Based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia with a group of professionals working as a team.

BIGMOMMA is a project dedicated to provide cheap and delicious home cooked food as well as food from local vendors right to your doorstep. All at the tip of your finger! With cheap and reasonable price offered, we believe the rise in cost of living will not stop anyone to enjoy tasty food.

Food lovers can order anytime they want daily and the food will be delivered according to estimated time provided in the application. Easy to track and use, BIGMOMMA is a GO for those who can spare no time but in dire need to satisfy the hunger nor for those who just simply crave for food.
